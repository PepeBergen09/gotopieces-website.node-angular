RegnCon

Written by Kristine Eide, 16/10/2019

RegnCon


This past weekend, GotoPiecesGames attended our local gaming convention, RegnCon XXVII for the first time. RegnCon is a Norwegian three day Convention that focus on role playing games, miniature and board games, and we were invited to playtest and show Fjordar to fellow board gamers and friends. During its´ 26 first years, RegnCon was held in a local school, but this year the Convention had taken a big step upwards, and moved into a fancy conference hotel. This was much appreciated with free popcorn, ice cream, sweet pastry and coffee that helped on the, at times, low blood sugar after hours of playing. The organizers had taken the complete board game collection from the Bergen library for people to play, in addition to people bringing their own games. As the evening evolved we could hear the buzz from local board gamers playing games in many conference rooms, and it was almost like being in a conference room during SPIEL, Essen.

Since we have played Fjordar non-stop for the past weeks and months, we decided to play something else for a change, and started the weekend off with a 5 player game of Terraforming Mars with expansions. Maybe not the quickest game we could have picked, but it’s definitively one of our favourite games and it was great to just play for fun again!

From 10 am on Saturday, we had people waiting to try Fjordar, and we got some really useful feedback that we will use in the further game development. Thank you all so much! People were intrigued by the theme as the Civil War in Norway is not something Norwegians know much of. We also got really good feedback on the ”push your luck” combat system, the modular, asymmetric, 3D game board and the church track. We see that people enjoy building and even more, burning churches!

We got to play some other games during the weekend as well; Eclipse, Black Out Hong Kong, Newton, Robin Hood and Arkham Horror. We also got to try an old classic deduction game, Secret Hitler. For some reason, none of us had played it before, but this is definitely a game we want to play again!
