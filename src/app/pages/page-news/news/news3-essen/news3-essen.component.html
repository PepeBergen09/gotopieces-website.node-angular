Essen 2019

Written by Kristine Eide, 31/10/2019

Essen 2019


Frode and Kristine have come home from our first SPIEL representing Go to Pieces Games, and what a week! Go to Pieces Games is a member of the Norwegian Tabletop Game Publishers Association www.norwaytabletop.weebly.com and at this SPIEL we shared a booth with other Norwegian Indie Publishers.

On Wednesday we used most of the day building our booth. It was a wonderful experience to be in the fair the day before the big day. The atmosphere was great, although a bit hectic as everyone was driving around, putting things together and unpacking big boxes, but there were also some time to have a look around and meet new people.



During the evening we attended Daniel Zayas´ Halloween themed social mixer. One of the best things regarding the board game community is its people. People are so open, positive and outgoing and this event was a great way to meet other people in the industry. We shared a demo table at the booth with Equity Board game and Viking Jarl. Thursday, Friday and Saturday we had a 3 – 4 hour session each day were we got to show and demo Fjordar for the international crowd.



Gotopiecesgames and SB Games.



SPIEL is the convention with the highest number of new releases. Kristine was therefore slightly worried that the crowds would not have time to stop for only a demo, as everyone would be rushing to get the latest releases. One thing we have gotten feedback on is that Fjordar has a great table presence and is a showstopper. This was soon obvious as we constantly had people stopping to learn about the game, sign up for the e-mail list and take photos. We even had several people how wanted to play the whole game.

Sunday was used to talking to talking to possible manufacturers. As we only had one demo session per day, we also had time to walk around to try other games, hang with new and old friends, do some shopping and eat German fast food.

What was the best experience at this years SPIEL?


Frode: The best thing was definitely watching how much people enjoyed playing Fjordar. One of my favourite things to do during SPIEL is hanging in the hotel conference room in the evenings, play games, punch games, have some beers, meet friends and look at the new buys.

Kristine: I 100 % agree with what Frode said. I would also like to mention that it was so nice meeting Vital Lacerda, as he is one of my favourite designers. It was also so nice to see again all the people you normally only meet a few times a year at board game conventions.



What were the 3 best games at SPIEL?


Frode: Terramarra, Tiny Towns and Fuji Koro.

Kristine: Tiny Towns is probably my best buy. I also enjoyed The Magnificent and Twelve heroes (best 5 euros spent ever!). I would also like to mention Escape Plan, but I have not had a chance to get it on the table yet.
